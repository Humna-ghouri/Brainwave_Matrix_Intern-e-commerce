Our Story

Crafting timeless pieces that celebrate artistry and tradition since 2015

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in a small studio in Jaipur, SNAPCART began with a simple mission: to preserve traditional craftsmanship while creating beautiful, functional pieces for modern living.

What started as a passion project between three friends has grown into a thriving community of over 200 artisans across India, each bringing their unique skills in jewelry making, perfumery, candle crafting, and leatherwork.
